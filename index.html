Our Work

Compartmental Modeling of Microparasitic Infectious Diseases such as COVID-19

Maharshi R. Pandya, Akshay R. Kapadia

05/10/2020

With the prevalence of coronavirus, it has become more important to take decisive actions based on quantitative data. Mathematical models can help decision makers implement effective strategies to mitigate the spread of such diseases. It is important to note that these models are based on specific assumptions and therefore do not convey any clinical advice but instead provide invaluable information to administrators. This information is especially important in times like these when the dynamics of diseases such as COVID-19 are somewhat unknown.

About Us


Our Mission

At Thalamos, we are determined in improving the current situation of global healthcare by utilizing cutting edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence and Big Data. We are committed to solving complex challenges and leading data-driven innovation to change the face of global medicine.

Why It's Important

Currently there is a large gap between rural clinics and large healthcare systems in terms of quality of care. The leading AI focused companies have only partnered with the giants in the healthcare industry so there is still a need for AI technology at smaller, less accessible locations. We truly believe that high quality healthcare should be easily accessible to everyone. Therefore, our AI offerings will be open source and available for implementation in a wide range of locations from small town clinics to large healthcare systems.
