About Us


Our Mission

At Thalamos, we are determined to improve the current situation of global healthcare by utilizing cutting edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and big data. We are committed to solving these complex problems and leading this AI revolution through data-driven innovation to change the face of global medicine.

Why It's Important

Currently there is a large gap between rural clinics and large healthcare systems in terms of quality of care. The leading AI focused companies have only partnered with the giants in the healthcare industry so there is still a need for AI technology at smaller, less accessible locations. We truly believe that high quality healthcare should be easily accessible to everyone. Therefore, our AI offerings will be open source and available for implementation in a wide range of locations from small town clinics to large healthcare systems.
